# Cookly
Cookly is a social media app for people who want to share, or discover new recipes.

## What I've learned
Building Cookly was an incredibly ambitious and rewarding endeavor. While the project is not without its flaws, the development journey provided me with invaluable, hands-on experience in real-world software engineering. One of my biggest takeaways is the vital importance of early and thorough integration planning—a lesson that will undoubtedly streamline my future projects. For a deeper dive into the specific technical insights and challenges overcome during this journey, please refer to the READMEs located in each component's directory.
